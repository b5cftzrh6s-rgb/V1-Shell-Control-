5. Temperature Compensation

Applies when Temperature column is selected. Simple linear correction for slurry viscosity temperature dependence.

Typical for zircon/colloidal silica slurries: 2.0 – 4.0. Measure your slurry at two temps to determine exact value if needed.
Formula: Compensated = Measured × (1 + (coeff/100) × (T_ref − T_meas))
Adjusts viscosity upward if measured hot (viscosity drops with higher temp).
